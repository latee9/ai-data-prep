Every time I got a new dataset, I'd spend hours cleaning it before doing any real analysis. So I automated the most common tasks.

**Features:**
- ⚡ **Smart Auto-Clean** — analyzes your dataset and applies all relevant cleaning automatically (removes duplicates, fixes dates, fills missing values, trims whitespace)
- 🔒 **PII Detection & Masking** — auto-detects emails, phone numbers, credit cards and lets you mask/hash/remove them (GDPR compliant)

- 💬 **AI Chat** — type "remove rows where salary is empty" in plain English and it generates + runs the pandas code
- 🔧 **Column Ops** — split columns, merge columns, regex extract, change data types, remove outliers
- 🌍 **6 Languages** — English, Arabic, French, Spanish, German, Turkish
